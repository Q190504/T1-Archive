A MESSAGE FROM THE ARCHIVERS

HELLO!

Welcome to the ZOFGK Archive, composed of Videos, News and Media Articles, and all the games of this roster together throughout the years.

This archive is updated automatically after every new content and games that come available. This archive will continue as long as ZOFGK are still together as a roster.

We hope this is a helpful file for new and current fans who want to get to know more about ZOFGK and reminsce the different contents and games that they have gone through together.

THIS WEBSITE IS BEST USED ON A PC/LAPTOP/TABLET.

This archive may not be the most aesthetically pleasing, but it is an easier tool for us as we have walls of texts. The archive is also not 100% done, as we will be adding content from 2021, too. We hope for your understanding.

How to use this archive?

In the Videos page and Media Interviews/News Articles page, links are placed in the video title link. Before it, there's an indication if it has English subtitles or not in the original content. A link is attached under "YES" if it is subtitled by a third-party. The next tab shows which players are present in the content/interview.

Every row has a descriptions or highlights column to easily help you navigate what the content is about or what happened during the game.

You may also use the tags to help you find similar content grouped together. On your browser, you may press Ctrl+F to activate the search bar.

Please help us!

Report to the archivers if any links are broken. Please help us by spreading the link to a lot of people, too!

Thank you for your support and happy viewing!
